READING LIST — Collection Items
Assumes: 80x24 terminal, Phosphor theme (green-on-black)
================================================================================

COLOR KEY:
  [green]   = bright green #00ff00 (ANSI 46) — unread item titles
  [bright]  = slightly brighter green (ANSI 48) — content links
  [cyan]    = bright cyan #00ffff (ANSI 51) — interactive accent, cache badge
  [dim]     = dark green #005f00 (ANSI 22-28) — borders, decorative
  [med]     = medium green #00af00 (ANSI 34) — domains, read items, secondary
  [white]   = white #ffffff (ANSI 15) — selected item text
  [sel-bg]  = dark green bg (ANSI 22) — selection background
  [pink]    = hot pink #ff0087 (ANSI 198) — podcast accent (rare)

================================================================================
INTERACTION NOTES
================================================================================

Navigation:
- j/k or ↓/↑: move between items
- Enter: open selected article in reader view
- d: remove item from collection (no confirmation)
- X: clear all items (Shift+X, confirmation prompt)
- J/K (Shift+j/k): reorder item up/down
- p: generate podcast from collection
- :: enter command mode
- b: go back to collection list
- ?: show help

Read/unread:
- Items start as ● unread
- Opening and reading marks as ○ read
- Read items: title in [med] (dimmer), circle ○ in [med]
- Unread items: title in [green] (bright), circle ● in [green]

Cache indicators:
- " · cached" in [cyan] after domain when article content is pre-loaded
- Status bar shows overall cache progress with ▰/▱ bar
- Cache warming happens in background when you enter the collection
