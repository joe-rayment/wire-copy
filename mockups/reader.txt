================================================================================
INTERACTION NOTES
================================================================================

Navigation:
- j/k or ↓/↑: scroll one line at a time
- Ctrl+D / Ctrl+U: scroll half-page down/up
- g: go to top
- G: go to bottom
- /: search in article text
- n/N: next/previous search result
- h/l: decrease/increase content width
- s: save article to reading list
- o: open in external browser
- v: switch to link tree view
- b: go back to previous page
- R: force refresh from network

Focus indicator:
- ▎ appears at a fixed position in the viewport (roughly 1/3 from top)
- As you scroll, the indicator stays put and content flows past it
- Color: [dim] dark green — subtle, non-distracting

Width adjustment:
- h: narrow by 4 chars (min 48)
- l: widen by 4 chars (max terminal width - 4)
- Width persists per session via UserSettingsStore
- Status bar updates W## indicator in real time
